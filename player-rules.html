How to Play

Review the rules and register your details to begin.

K.S. Rangasamy College of Technology

Objective

Complete all puzzles in the shortest possible time while following the event rules.

Basic Rules

  • Read every puzzle carefully before submitting.
  • Do not refresh, minimize, or close the browser while playing.
  • Each set of answers is final unless organizers allow retries.
  • Collaborating or sharing answers is prohibited unless stated otherwise.
  • Follow any additional instructions from the coordinators.

Scoring

  • Correct answers add to your score.
  • Wrong answers may add no points or incur penalties depending on event configuration.
  • Final rank depends on accuracy plus your total time.

* Exact scoring and time limits can differ per event. Confirm details with organizers.
